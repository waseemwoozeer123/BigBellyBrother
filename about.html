Big Belly Brother

Team BBB take your tastebuds on a gourmet round-the-globe trip in Leicester.

The city is justifiably famous for its mouth-watering curries – the Golden Mile is a must-visit for curry lovers! As a former Curry Capital of Britain winner, Leicester is home to a range of Asian cuisine, from traditional thali’s at Bobby’s to Indo-Chinese delicacies at Mirch Masala and Kerala seafood specials at Kayal in the city centre.

If Indian food isn’t what you’re after – don’t worry! Whether you fancy a fine dining experience at The Case, the exotic South American flavours of Bodega Cantina or the authentic taste of Tuscany at OGGI Simply Italian… even the most discerning diners will be satisfied with the range of choice in the city.

Did you know that Leicester is home to over 60 vegetarian eateries? The city’s restaurants are a treat for veggies and vegans, and even meat eaters might be converted after sampling the delights on offer!
