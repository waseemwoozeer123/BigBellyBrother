Bodega Cantina ★★★★

Bodega is a contemporary Cantina bringing fresh, homemade South American inspired cuisine to Leicester. Bodega offer one the best drinks selections in the city, with mouth-watering cocktails, a handpicked range of South American wine and a comprehensive selection of regional bottled beers. Fresh, authentic, easy going South American food is what they are all about! It’s not just about Mexican food either – not when other South American countries also offer so much variety... the Bodega team have travelled far and wide to bring the authentic tastes of the continent to your plate.

By Waseem Woozeer 12 comments

Chutney Ivy ★★★★★

The Michelin Guide-listed Chutney Ivy’s serves a delicious menu of pan-Indian food – if you’re looking for traditional Indian cuisine with a modern twist, Chutney Ivy is the place to visit. What does that look like? Expect all the things you love about Indian food – the aromatic spices, the diverse flavours and the colourful dishes – but brought up to date with modern food trends like crispy duck samosas or salmon tikka massala. The menu, which has been designed from years of research by chefs, is a real journey around the Indian subcontinent, from the Bengal and Goa to the traditional Muglai flavours of north India. With a selection of meat, fish and vegetarian dishes, every taste is catered for. The restaurants pre/post theatre banquet is a sight to behold and its handy location just across the road from Curve theatre makes it the ideal stop when seeing a show.

By Jamie Vardy 82 comments
