Okay, so what are we about?!

BigBellyBrother, a food blog based in Leicester, where we showcase some of Leicester local's finest.

Saints Of Mokha

Fell in love with the place as soon as I walked in. I wanted something appropriate for breakfast and they had cookies, croissants or a smoothie bowl. Opted for the smoothie bowl apprehensively, expecting just a bowl of mushed fruits blended with ice. The presentation was too cute so eased off some of the apprehension. However, tucking into it there was a generous layer of granola which gave it a pleasant crunch! So top marks for that! The coffee was really good.. I'm a bit of a sweet tooth so let the host decide what was good and coffee was just as enjoyable.

Kobe Sizzlers

I have been many times to Kobe since it has been open. Everytime the staff were friendly and welcoming. Service is always fast. I have tried most of the burgers on the menu and they are truly an experience - in fact, I find it difficult to order burgers anywhere else as Kobe has set the standard to a very high bar!
